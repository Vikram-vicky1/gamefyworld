Content creation

We supply hologram technology as well as animations for holograms. The media module within Power 3D application can output in real-time and directly into holograms. An interactive experience, immersive and innovative visualization, requiring no special glasses. Holograms sizes can vary, going from fit for mobile phones to big seized ones, of up to 6x2 meters, which can interactively present a full-scale automobile or large real estate projects. Besides the usual presentation of products or systems, we can implement interactive customization tools, through Configurators 3D application.
