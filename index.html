Media and email module

Offer your visitors the option to take their own photo and video captures, of any moments they decide on, and then email themselves what catches their eye, directly from the application. Within GDPR rules and with their consent only, you are also able to gather details about the visitors and the products they were interested in and use them to subsequently keep them informed. Take an interactive approach and replace the classic 3D animations with the captures taken directly from the application, useful in creating your own movies and becoming your own screenwriter, with photos taken from all possible angles. More – The application is an interactive simulator, providing overall ‘how it works’ videos for users. The experience is guided by attractive 3D design, 3d explosion animations, transparency effects, real time simulations and personalized content. It can integrate almost any gamification feature and allows potential customers to explore everything, from any angle, to move the displayed items around, zoom in or out, explore how is made both inside and out, to visualize color highlighted forces or flows.

Photo Capture

The user can at any time capture a photo of what is being designed, using the dedicated input (capture) and output (save) buttons available on the application’s interface.

Video Capture

A special developed module within the application that allows captures to be done at different resolutions, lower one more appropriate for a movie which is to be emailed to the stand visitors; a higher resolution (full HD or 4k) can be chosen if it is intended for digital advertising material creation.

Email Module

You can email your visitors or customers directly from the application and make use of the multiple options, such as sending out any photos or videos captured during the demo. After pressing the email button and before selecting any attachment options (photo, video, company’s pdf booklet etc), the user is prompted to consent to receiving emails from the company hosting the demo presentation. The email can be sent right away or scheduled for a more convenient time (for example, when a better internet connection in available). The activity traceability solutions integrated in the application will contribute to an improved suggestion bar, to a better population and extension internal data base and efficient email campaigns, as the platform stores all emails and their correspondent content (screenshots, contact details etc).

Security module

We integrated more than 40 security features to ensure that access rights can be set up at various levels or per features, taking into account data sensitivity and confidentiality. Implicitly, the security levels can be configured per user profile, be it internal or external. Running on kiosk mode does not allow access to other applications from PC, the internal content is strongly secured (products, features, etc.), control through secure touchscreen keyboard. Contact us for more details

NFC/RFID Magnetic Card module

Manage the application accessing and traceability logistics with the help of NFC/RFID systems, by simply attaching an NFC device to a PC USB socket. The magnetic card access control systems allow thousands of users management and different access right levels mapping, correlated with product or specific components visibility, email sending, users actions traceability etc Practically, in an exhibition set up more with a team of 200 people involved, you can show in the public area only certain features of the displayed products. A separate and dedicated showcase can be conducted in a private location, based on customer profile - user level and access rights correlated, partially or fully disclosure of details. NFC module is also applicable for training or school classes monitoring and coordination (employees or students pairing evolution tracking etc) or for starting a certain presentation upon a visitor’s entering a particular area in a museum, by accessing the numerical control machine connected with a PC etc

Web and cloud module

Efficiently map your user network and create customized user profiles, internal and external, grant full or limited access rights. The module also allows setting up multiple email addresses, which can be used by the users with the appropriate access rights to email the stand visitors or your customers.
