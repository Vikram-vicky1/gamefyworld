Interactive Booth

Playground for wise ideas

Gamify and showcase your concept, boost your visiblilty in exhibitions, events or shops. We integrate in our presentation projects the gamification concept, digital twin and 3D modelling to enable the simualation of the physical orginal in digit space, along witn high-quality visual effects(animatios, exploded views, transparency, color highlighted flows or statuses, fog etc) and dedicated professional equipment, to engage customers in complete and like tours. Great built-in features make the power 3D application adaptable to diversified representations, for ideas and products across various sectors, allowing real time simualation and automatic design configuration. Visitors can go through a step by step discovery and immersive experience. We can provide the dedicated equipment for building the entire experience, with videowall projections, interactive models, interactive light panels, Holograms and holographic systems, touchscreens, AR/VR and mixed reality tech support.

you can make everything even more memorable on both sides with use of applications modules and features. Media and email module for sending out for any photos or videos captured during the demo. It will allow your Visitors as well as to yourrself the option to take photo and video captures, of any moments they or you decided on, and then email everything directly from the application. On consent only, details about the Visitors and the products they were interested in will be gathered and used to subsequently keep them informed. The Administrative module helps in efficiently map your user network, where you can create customized user network, where you can create customized user profiles, grant full or limited access rights.
